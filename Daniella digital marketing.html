About me


My name is Daniella Murphy i am 22 years old From a young age, I has always had a keen eye for capturing the beauty in the world around them. At just 12 years old, they discovered a passion for photography, often borrowing their family's camera to snap pictures of anything that caught their interest. Whether it was the vibrant colors of a sunset, the intricate details of a flower, or candid moments with friends and family, I found joy in freezing these moments in time. However, it wasn't until they turned 18 that this hobby transformed into a true calling. With the gift of their first professional camera, I began to delve deeper into the art and science of photography. They spent countless hours learning about different techniques, experimenting with lighting, and understanding the nuances of composition. This period marked a significant turning point, as they started to see photography not just as a pastime, but as a powerful medium for storytelling and expression. Throughout these formative years, I honed their skills by taking on various projects, from portrait sessions to landscape photography. They also explored the world of digital editing, enhancing their images to bring out the best in each shot. Their dedication and passion for photography have led them to create a diverse portfolio that showcases their unique perspective and artistic vision. In addition to their technical prowess, I have a natural ability to connect with their subjects, making them feel at ease in front of the camera. This talent has been particularly evident in their portrait work, where they capture not just the physical appearance, but the essence and personality of the individuals they photograph. Now, at 22, I continues to pursue their love for photography with unwavering enthusiasm. They are constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to grow as an artist. Whether it's through personal projects, collaborations, or professional assignments, I am committed to pushing the boundaries of their creativity and sharing their unique vision with the world.
